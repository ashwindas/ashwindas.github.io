Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2024

This privacy policy describes how Ashwin Das Gururaja's personal website collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit this website.

Information Collection

This website collects limited information to improve user experience:

Google Analytics

With your consent, this website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our content. This helps improve the site experience. Google Analytics collects:

  • Anonymous usage data (pages visited, time spent, interactions)
  • Device and browser information
  • Geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Referring websites

All IP addresses are anonymized, and no personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out of analytics tracking at any time.

GitHub Pages Hosting

The website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect certain log data such as:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Time/date of visit
  • Referring website

Use of Information

Google Analytics: Anonymous analytics data is used solely to understand website usage patterns and improve user experience. This data is processed by Google according to their privacy policy.

GitHub Pages: Any information collected by GitHub Pages is subject to their privacy policy and is not accessible by the website owner. It is primarily used for troubleshooting and hosting purposes.

Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses:

  • Necessary cookies: Theme preference (light/dark mode) - always active
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics tracking - only with your consent
  • Consent preferences: Your cookie consent choices - stored locally

None of these cookies track you across websites or collect personal information.

Your Choices

You have full control over analytics tracking:

  • Choose to accept or decline analytics when you first visit
  • Change your preferences at any time
  • Analytics data is anonymous and IP addresses are anonymized
  • No tracking occurs without explicit consent

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting. See GitHub's Privacy Statement
  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics (with consent). See Google's Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The date of the latest revision will always be stated at the top of this page.

Contact Information
